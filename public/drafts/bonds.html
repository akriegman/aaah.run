Tax Bonds

26 September 2025

I've been thinking lately that there should be a way to invest in people, so that we can have institutions Idea: the government sells "tax bonds", allowing private companies to invest in the success of individuals. Your taxes go to the owners of your tax bonds instead of to the government. The government gets their money from the IPO on each new citizen. If a health insurance company owns tax bonds in their clients, then they have an incentive to actually keep you healthy. If a social media company owns tax bonds in their users, then they have an incentive to focus your attention on things that matter, instead of giving you an eating disorder or shaming you into "being informed". Educating children is all of a sudden profitable. You can buy tax bonds in a toddler, educate them, and sell the bonds for a profit. Maybe this could combat the birth rate crisis? Since there's a way to profit off of children in the short term... maybe if we give the child's bonds to the parents' bond holders... this would take the government out of the loop...
